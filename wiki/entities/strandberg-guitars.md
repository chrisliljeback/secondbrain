# Strandberg Guitars

Swedish maker of premium **headless** guitars and basses (styled `.strandberg*`),
built around ergonomics, light weight, and extended-range playability. Tagline
theme: *"Born in Sweden, played worldwide"* / *"Feel better, sound better."*
Christoffer's ([[christoffer-liljeback]]) current employer. Based in Uppsala, Sweden.

## Positioning
Headless guitars "scientifically engineered for comfort," featuring extended range
(6/7/8-string) and multi-scale models, plus 4- and 5-string basses. Core promise is
[[headless-guitar-ergonomics]]: lightweight, ergonomic, "engineered to help you play
more." Instruments are notably light — some N2 Metal models ~2.5 kg / ~5.5 lbs.

## Product lines (series)
- **[[boden-n2]]** — the current flagship platform. Standard N2, Original N2, and
  Metal N2 variants; curved N2 body top.
- **Essential** — accessible entry series (e.g. Boden Essential 6, ~15,399 SEK).
- **Boden Metal** — heavy-music focused; POWR:D pickups by Fishman.
- **Artist Signature** — signature models (e.g. Plini).
- Also: Standard, Original, Jazz, Prog, Classic, Futur, J Artisan, Special Editions.

## Signature technology
- **EndurNeck** — the trapezoidal/faceted neck profile; often Titanium-reinforced
  Roasted Maple. Core to the ergonomic story.
- **[[arc-tilt-tremolo]]** (Arc TILT) — twin-incline-lever tremolo system launched
  2026 for tuning stability and smooth feel; on Boden Standard N2T / Original N2T.
- **EGS hardware**, e.g. EGS Arc hardtail bridge.
- **POWR:D pickups** — reverse-mounted, by Fishman, with Precision/Dynamic voicings.

## Market presence
- Instagram @strandbergguitars: ~254K followers, 5,441+ posts.
- Strong artist/community orbit: Plini, Guthrie Govan, Jordan Rudess (jcrudess),
  Rhythm Shaw, and others regularly featured/tagged.
- Direct-to-consumer via strandbergguitars.com; web-exclusive colorways;
  outlet + campaign discounts (e.g. "Making Room" up to 20% off NX models).
- Customer service highly rated (95% give top rating per site testimonials).

## Company financials
(Per Allabolag/UC company registry, FY2024. Legal entity: **Strandberg Guitars
AB**, org.nr 556889-2862, founded 2012, Uppsala. Part of the **Cithara BidCo AB**
group; CEO Markus Nils Olof Engelbrekts; board incl. founder Ola Erik Strandberg.)
- Revenue 110,989 kSEK (~111M SEK); EBITDA 17,652 kSEK; net result for the year
  −41 kSEK (near break-even after allocations/tax).
- Solidity 63.5% (up sharply from 46.9% in 2023 — strong balance sheet). Profit
  margin 16% (down from 17.6% in 2023).
- 14 registered employees.
- **Read against [[strandberg-web-performance]]**: online (DTC) revenue is
  ~13.6M SEK YTD — a fraction of the ~111M SEK total. Most revenue still runs
  through dealers/wholesale, not the web store.
