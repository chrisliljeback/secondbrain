## Reading it
- N2T tremolo colorways ([[arc-tilt-tremolo]] models) dominate both traffic and
  revenue — consistent with [[arc-tilt-tremolo]] being 2026's best-selling line.
- Checkout abandonment is the one metric moving the wrong way (+32pp YoY) —
  worth investigating (price/shipping surprise, payment friction) before
  spending more on top-of-funnel content.
- High-view/zero-sale SKUs in the 7-day window (Essential 6 Transformative Teal,
  Standard N2.6T Electric Fuchsia) may be past a hype peak or blocked on
  stock/price — check before commissioning new content for them, per
  [[content-decision-framework]].
- Cross-check with [[strandberg-guitars]]'s company financials: this channel's
  13.6M SEK YTD is a fraction of the ~111M SEK FY2024 total revenue — most
  revenue still runs through dealers/wholesale, not DTC.
