# Boden N2

The current flagship guitar platform from [[strandberg-guitars]], built on the
curved **N2 body** design. Available in 6-, 7-, and 8-string configurations.

## Variants
- **Standard N2** — the core modern line. Reviewed as "you might be surprised how
  much better you play" and "sci-fi quality."
- **Original N2** — higher-tier. Reviewed as "one of the most intuitive guitars
  I've ever had the pleasure of playing" despite the headless design and
  [[headless-guitar-ergonomics|EndurNeck]] profile.
- **Standard N2T / Original N2T** — the "T" variants carry the [[arc-tilt-tremolo]]
  system (vs. hardtail).
- **Standard N2 Metal POWR:D** — limited metal-focused drop: solid Basswood body,
  EGS Arc hardtail, Titanium-reinforced Roasted Maple EndurNeck, Ebonol fretboard,
  reverse-mounted POWR:D by Fishman pickups (Precision/Dynamic voicings),
  ~2.5 kg / ~5.5 lbs, Black Metallic Satin.

## Finishes (examples seen)
Blue Aura Metallic, Amber Haze Burst Gloss, Coral Burst, Electric Fuchsia Metallic
(N2.6T), Black Denim Burst (N2.6T), Black Metallic Satin (Metal).

## Significance to Christoffer's work
The N2 platform launch (2025-09) — for which [[christoffer-liljeback]] scripted and
produced the launch film, music videos, and photography in-house — won 2× Platinum
Award from Guitar World. The [[arc-tilt-tremolo]] launch on N2T models was the
#1 best-selling Strandberg of 2026.
