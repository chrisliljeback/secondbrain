# Headless guitar ergonomics

The core design philosophy behind [[strandberg-guitars]]: rethink how an instrument
*feels in use* rather than repeating tradition. The result is headless, lightweight,
and ergonomically shaped guitars marketed as "scientifically engineered for comfort"
and "engineered to help you play more."

## Key elements
- **Headless design** — removes the headstock, cutting weight and rebalancing the
  instrument. Reduces neck-dive and overall fatigue.
- **EndurNeck** — a faceted/trapezoidal neck profile that encourages a more relaxed,
  neutral hand position. Described by reviewers as "daunting" and "alien" at first
  but ultimately "as user-friendly as a guitar can get."
- **Curved N2 body top** — contoured to "melt into your body" (reviewer quote).
- **Low weight** — some models ~2.5 kg / ~5.5 lbs.

## Why it matters (positioning)
The ergonomic story is the brand's central differentiator and the emotional promise:
comfort → you play more → you play better ("Feel better, sound better"). Reviewers
consistently note improved playability: "you might be surprised how much better
you play." This is the through-line for most [[strandberg-guitars]] product
messaging and a recurring theme in [[christoffer-liljeback]]'s content work.
