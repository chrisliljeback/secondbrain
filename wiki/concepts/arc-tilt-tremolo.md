# Arc TILT tremolo

A tremolo system from [[strandberg-guitars]], launched in 2026, built on
**Twin Incline Lever Technology** — a twin-axis tilt that reduces resistance so
motion feels smooth and controlled and returns predictably to pitch. Marketed for
"immaculate tuning stability, increased range, effortless flutter, and the
smoothest feel we've ever created."

It applies the brand's [[headless-guitar-ergonomics|"how it feels in use"]]
philosophy to the tremolo itself: rethinking movement rather than repeating a
traditional design.

## Where it ships
On the Boden Standard N2T and Boden Original N2T series (the "T" = tremolo variants
of [[boden-n2]]), launched with new finishes — e.g. Standard N2.6T Electric Fuchsia
Metallic and Original N2.6T Black Denim Burst.

## Significance
The Arc TILT launch (2026-01) was produced by [[christoffer-liljeback]] — a 3D
mechanism explainer plus a directed artist film with an original score, launching
two guitar models at once. **Outcome: #1 best-selling Strandberg of 2026.** A strong
case study in explaining a mechanically complex product through 3D + film.
