# Source: About Christoffer — AI Collaboration Operating Manual

Christoffer's own standing operating manual for how AI (Claude/Cowork) should work
with him. Designed to stay valid for years — not a status log. Explicitly separate
from `memory.md` (day-to-day project status, not part of this wiki).

## Key takeaways
- **Role & context**: video/content producer at [[strandberg-guitars]]; dropping to
  60% at Strandberg from fall 2026 → explicit push toward lean, low-maintenance
  systems over clever ones.
- **Business context at Strandberg**: sales precedes brand experimentation; activate
  existing inventory before new production; budgets are constrained (assume no
  extra spend); every content decision must support sales, e-com conversion,
  launch execution, dealer enablement, or inventory activation — or justify why not.
- **Decision framework** (ranked): business impact → reusability → production
  speed → visual quality → novelty (last, never sufficient alone).
- **Working style**: wants finished solutions, not ideas to react to; one strong
  recommendation over many options; kill weak ideas early; judgment over
  brainstorming.
- **Leadership dynamics**: CMO wants recommendation + decision needed, nothing
  else; CEO values finished work/systems over status updates.
- **Creative standards**: premium execution, restraint, timeless design, strong
  typography, realistic production value, one idea per frame, systems over
  one-offs.
- **Career direction**: shifting from hands-on producer toward building production
  systems / creative ops / workflow design — the differentiator is systems, not
  shooting.
- **Self-identified blind spots**: reassurance-seeking messages, over-explaining,
  showing work too early, misreading silence as negative, scope creep, polishing
  low-value work, building before validating value.
- **Tools & stack**: Claude Cowork, Figma (MCP), Favro (kanban), SharePoint (asset
  storage), GitHub (`chrisliljeback/launch-hub`), custom apps (Ad Creator, Launch
  Hub, Product Image Editor, Content Hub — Vite+React+Electron), Canon R6.
- File org rule (Cowork-specific, not this vault): deliverables go to
  `Documents/Claude Cowork/Projects/[project]`; `/About me`, `/Skills`,
  `/Templates`, `/Plugins` are manually maintained reference folders.
