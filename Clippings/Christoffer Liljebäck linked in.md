## Om

Twenty years in film, content, and creative production — freelance, agency, in-house, global brand. Creative judgment and production structure aren't opposites: the job is holding both at once and still making something good. Today I run visual content at Strandberg Guitars — brand, e-commerce, launches, campaigns.  
  
Before that: seven years as a producer at Populate Film Agency, leading 200+ productions for Systembolaget, DeLaval, Electrolux, Regeringskansliet and Region Stockholm. My specialty was making complex things understandable on film — the Stockholm metro expansion, milking robots, industrial systems.  
  
I've also built an internal production function at WSP (70,000 employees) from scratch.  
  
I use AI throughout my workflow. It doesn't replace craft — it means I spend less time on the parts that don't require it… mer
