## Experience

Creative Production Manager / Senior Content Producer Strandberg Guitars · 2 yrs 11 mos · Present

I run visual content production end-to-end for a premium guitar brand with a global customer base — brief to delivery, across e-commerce, product launches, campaigns, and events.

That's 30+ productions a year. The standards are high, the timelines aren't generous, and things break if the process isn't solid. A big part of the job has been building the systems that keep quality consistent when everything is moving at once: how work gets prioritized, how briefs get written, how handoffs actually happen.

More recently I've been integrating AI into the production process — finding the gaps myself and building the fixes, mostly around scripting, copy, and workflow automation.

I work with internal teams and external partners, and a lot of my time is spent translating between what people want creatively and what's actually possible to deliver.

Senior Content Producer / Production Lead – Film & Visual Communication WSP Sweden · 2 yrs

Oct 2021 – Sep 2023 · Stockholm, Sweden · On-site

At WSP Sweden, I helped establish film and visual content production as a central communication tool for brand building, recruitment, and internal communication within a large, complex organization.

I worked hands-on with planning, producing, and quality-assuring film and visual content, while also developing production guidelines, workflows, and concepts to support consistent and efficient delivery.

A key part of the role was collaborating closely with communication, leadership, and external production partners, as well as contributing to the build-up of an internal studio environment for webinars, podcasts, and live formats.

Film Producer / Production Lead Populate · 7 yrs 3 mos

Aug 2014 – Oct 2021 · Greater Uppsala Area · On-site

At Populate Film Agency, I worked as a producer in a high-paced agency environment, leading and delivering over 200 film and content productions across brand, product, and campaign communication.

I was responsible for client dialogue, production planning, timelines, and delivery, working closely with creative teams and external production resources. Much of the work involved complex briefs within large organizations, requiring clear structure, prioritization, and the ability to translate business and communication needs into effective productions.

Clients included Electrolux, DeLaval, Sandvik, Atlas Copco, Systembolaget, and Region Stockholm.

Video & Content Producer (Freelance) Freelance · 10 yrs

Aug 2004 – Jul 2014 · Uppsala, Sweden

I worked as a freelance video and content producer, delivering a wide range of productions across commercial, cultural, and organizational communication. The role involved full production responsibility, tight deadlines, and managing multiple projects in parallel for a broad set of clients.

Selected clients included Barncancerfonden, SVT, Forsman & Bodenfors, Sony Music, Universal Music, and Astrid Lindgren Memorial Award.
