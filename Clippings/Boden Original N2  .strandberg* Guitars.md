**.strandberg\* POWR:D BY FISHMAN PICKUPS**

Only cutting-edge electronics can capture the clarity and dynamics of an Original N2, which is why these guitars come equipped with custom-developed American-made.strandberg\* powr:d by Fishman pickups with selectable voicing to cover tones from pristine clean to saturated hi gain with ease. The *Dynamic Voice* (Voice 1) feature medium output with a balanced frequency response slightly favoring bass and treble for an open and responsive tone, while the *Precision Voice* (Voice 2) has higher output and an elevated high midrange for a warmer and tighter character.

![](https://a.storyblok.com/f/320488/1400x1000/b9401960e5/original-n2-arched-body.png/m/3840x0/filters:quality(90))
